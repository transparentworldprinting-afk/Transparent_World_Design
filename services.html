Our Services

Providing innovative engineering solutions, technical expertise, and professional consultancy services to support sustainable development and successful project delivery.

Electrical Engineering Design

Design of low-voltage and medium-voltage electrical systems, power distribution networks, switchboards, protection systems, and electrical infrastructure for commercial and industrial facilities.

Solar Power System Design

Design of grid-connected, off-grid, and hybrid solar energy systems including photovoltaic arrays, inverters, battery storage, and energy management solutions.

Hybrid Energy Systems

Engineering design of integrated power systems combining solar, battery storage, diesel generators, and utility power to ensure reliable and efficient energy supply.

Telecommunications Network Design

Design and planning of telecommunications infrastructure including fiber optic networks, microwave systems, wireless communications, cellular networks, and data transmission systems.

Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) Systems

Design and integration of Automatic Transfer Switch systems for seamless transition between utility power, backup generators, and renewable energy sources.

Backup Power Solutions

Generator sizing, backup power system design, load analysis, and emergency power solutions for critical facilities and telecommunications sites.

Battery Energy Storage Systems

Design and implementation of battery storage systems for renewable energy projects, telecommunications facilities, and mission-critical operations.

Industrial Control Systems

Design of motor control centers, automation systems, industrial electrical controls, and monitoring systems for industrial applications.

Technical Consultancy

Professional engineering consultancy services including feasibility studies, system assessments, technical reviews, project planning, and engineering recommendations.
